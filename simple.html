How Reading Changes Your Perspective

Reading opens the door to diverse experiences, cultures, and viewpoints that you may never encounter in everyday life. It allows you to step into someone else’s shoes, fostering empathy, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence. Whether it’s fiction or non-fiction, books challenge your beliefs, broaden your understanding of the world, and help you see situations through a more informed and compassionate lens. Over time, this habit shapes your personality, deepens your insight, and encourages a more open-minded and reflective way of thinking.

Munamadan

Munamadan is a classic Nepali epic poem by Laxmi Prasad Devkota. It tells the story of Madan, who travels to Lhasa to earn money, leaving behind his beloved Muna. The poem explores love, sacrifice, patriotism, and the harsh realities of poverty and separation.
